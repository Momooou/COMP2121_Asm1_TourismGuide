Food

Award-winning steakhouse: Hardwick Hall Hotel-The Rib Room Steakhouse & Grill
 Succulent steaks from Hereford Beef, raised on Durham farms are the House Special. Meat is well matured from 28-32 days and can be viewed in the Rib Room meat locker.

The excellent menu is matched by a sensibly priced substantial wine list offering a wine to complement any menu choice as well as champagnes, dessert wines, port, armagnac and liquors.

Address: Sedgefield, County Durham, TS21 2EH

Comfy place for light meals: South Causey Inn & Causey Bubble Dining Pods
 Our beautiful gardens allow al fresco dining in the summer and log fires will keep you warm in the colder months of the year.

We welcome you to drop in after a country walk for breakfast with the family and 4 legged friends in our Bar and Snug area, or maybe homemade scones and cakes. Lunch and dinners are served 7 days a week and we have full a la carte as well as daily specials, Steak and Curry nights and the Great Giant Yorkshire Pudding Night!

Address: Beamish Burn Road, Stanley, County Durham, DH9 0LS

Accommodation for solo-travellers:

Strawberry Hill Farm Camping & Caravan Park
 If you are also a person who loves nature, wildlife, you may choose to stay in a campsite same as I did during my 3-months backpack trip to Durham!

This is a photo of one of the campsite I stayed for weeks. It is a big campsite, surrounded by a big field, together with other campsitemates.

Although it is near the fields, it is also close to the bus stations. It is quite convenient to travel around.
Other than that, I hanged out with my campsitemates, they were very kind. We surely shared many great moments there!

The following facilities are provided at the campsite:

  1. Modern shower block with hot showers
  2. Shaver points
  3. Laundry washing machine
  4. Tumble dryer
  5. Book Corner
  6. Freezer
  7. On-site Tea Room
  8. Small shop supplying basic groceries, icecream and BBQ and camping supplies.
  9. gas exchange
  10. Outdoor seating area
  11. Full facilities for the disabled

If you need anything else, contact the campsite owner and he will be willing to help!

Feel free to check this link out for more details about the campsite:
https://strawberry-hill-farm.co.uk/
